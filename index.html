About Me

Description

Recently, I made a career switch from the healthcare industry to the tech industry.

After a stringent interview process, I took a job at Infin8 Sdn Bhd in Kuala Lumpur as a Junior Project Manager.

I am also a graduate of the NEXT Academy full-stack web development bootcamp where I learnt both front-end and back-end coding languages.

Wait.... what? Learnt coding and you become a project manager and not a web developer? heh?

I understand it may be a bit confusing, but being a web developer is not the only career pathway after the web development bootcamp.

During our final project at NEXT, I discovered that I felt very motivated in planning out the project timelines and features and also delegating tasks to my team members.

Initially my plan was to become a web developer first to develop my coding skills and logic, and then eventually become a project manager. The reason behind this is that I would further understand the time required to build a feature and also determine the difficulty of a feature, thus managing timelines better.

Thankfully, Infin8 decided to give me a chance to start out straightway as a junior project manager and learn coding on the side during my free time.

I am looking forward to my new adventure in life :D
